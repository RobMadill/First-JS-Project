ROBERT MADILL

WELCOME TO MY GAME OF ROCK-PAPER-SCISSORS

THIS IS HOW TO PLAY:

You will be asked to choose either: Rock (Press 1), Paper (Press 2) or Scissors (Press 3)
When you enter your choice and click OK, the computer will randomly choose Rock, Paper or Scissors as well
If you win, you'll get a point. If the computer wins, it will get a point. Try to get as many points as you can to beat the computer!
After each round, you will be asked if you would like to play again. If you decide to end the game (Click cancel), you will see the final score!
